NO ELECTRICAL PARTS

The beauty of the Classy Horn is its effortless simplicity. The speaker is purely mechanical and hence does not rely on any electrical source. The acoustically enhancing inner shape of the Classy Horn amplifies the sound emitted from your smartphone by an impressive 400%.

No power cords, cables, batteries or electrical parts, enables hassle-free installation and complete mobility. What’s more, interruptions with USB and smartphone inserts, Wi-Fi or Bluetooth connectivity, draining batteries or searching for a power source are problems that will never be associated with the Classy Horn.

Enjoy unlimited music play time and with zero disruptions.

UNIVERSAL HOLDER

The Classy Horn’s one of a kind universal holder is compatible with over 17,000 smartphone models. So regardless of your smartphone brand or model preferences, the Classy Horn is completely utilizable.

Classy horns are so simple to use that anyone from the young to elderly can use them with minimal effort. Simply insert your smartphone into the universal holder for your daily music pleasure and teleconference needs.

OUR DESIGNS

Classy horns are a distinguished and unique addition to any space. Available in an array of contemporary designs in a selection of premium wood and metal veneers, leathers and color schemes that will complement the interior aesthetics and personality of your space.

The Classy Horn can be used from the comfort of your bedroom, living room, dining area, office and garden while adding a stylish and unique finish. Your on-the-go Classy speaker is also the ideal functional accessory to be used at the beach, on picnics and anywhere adventure calls.
